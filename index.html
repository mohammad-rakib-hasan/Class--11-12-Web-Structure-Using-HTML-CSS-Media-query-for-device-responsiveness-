Slovenia

Ljubljana, the capital of Slovenia, is a picturesque city that combines
charming architecture with a lively cultural scene. The city's Old Town is a pedestrian
friendly area with narrow streets, beautiful bridges, and colorful buildings.
The iconic Dragon Bridge and the triple bridge are highlights, while
Ljubljana Castle offers panoramic views of the city.
The lush Tivoli Park provides a green oasis, making Ljubljana a
perfect blend of natural beauty and urban charm.

Switzerland

Ucerne, nestled in the heart of Switzerland, is a city that exudes beauty and charm.
Surrounded by snow-capped mountains and sitting beside Lake Lucerne, this city offers
breathtaking views in every direction. The Chapel Bridge, a 14th-century wooden bridge
adorned with flowers, and the Water Tower are iconic landmarks. The old town is filled
with colorful frescoed buildings and historic churches. For a stunning view, take a boat
trip on the lake or ride the funicular to Mount Pilatus. Lucerne’s blend of natural and
architectural beauty makes it a must-visit.
